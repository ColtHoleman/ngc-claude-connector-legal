Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how NGC General Contractors ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information through the Claude Connector application ("the App"), which integrates with QuickBooks Online for internal project management purposes.

Information We Collect

The App accesses the following information from your QuickBooks Online account:

  • Project names and identifiers
  • Customer/client names for project organization
  • Basic company information necessary for API authentication

How We Use Information

Information accessed through the App is used solely for:

  • Creating and managing projects in QuickBooks Online
  • Enabling employee time tracking to specific projects
  • Internal business operations of NGC General Contractors

Data Storage and Security

Authentication tokens are stored locally on authorized devices only. We do not store QuickBooks data on external servers. All API communications use industry-standard HTTPS encryption.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your QuickBooks data with third parties. Data is used exclusively for internal business operations.

Your Rights

You may disconnect the App from your QuickBooks account at any time through the QuickBooks Online settings, which will revoke all access.
